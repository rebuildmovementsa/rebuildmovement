Our Mission

Recovery doesn't end
at the clinic door

In South Africa, thousands of people go through rehab every year — but most relapse within months. Not because treatment doesn't work, but because recovery doesn't continue. There's nowhere to go after.

Recovery today is clinical. It focuses on stopping the addiction, but not on rebuilding a life. What people actually need is structure, identity, and community. Because if nothing changes in your life, you go back to what you know.

Rebuild Movement is a non-profit fitness community built specifically for people in recovery. We run group training sessions — bodyweight training, functional fitness, running — in a sober, supportive, judgment-free environment.

We're designed to complement the mental and spiritual work of the NA programme — adding the physical element that makes recovery whole.

🧱 Free to anyone in recovery — always

What We Stand For

The Three Pillars

Everything we do is built on three foundational beliefs about recovery and fitness.

🧠

Mind-Body Connection

Physical movement directly impacts mental health. We design every session with both in mind — building emotional regulation alongside physical strength.

🤝

Community & Accountability

You don't have to rebuild alone. Our community is made up of people who understand the path — a space of genuine support, zero judgment, and shared purpose.

📐

Structure & Routine

If nothing changes in your life, you go back to what you know. A consistent fitness routine becomes an anchor — a healthy ritual that grounds each day.

How It Works

The Rebuild Program

01

Show Up — 48 Hours Sober

That's the only requirement. No fitness level, no prior experience, no cost. Just show up. We take care of the rest.

02

Group Training Sessions

Bodyweight training, functional fitness, running, and mobility & stretching in a sober, supportive environment. No judgment. No pressure. Just consistency, discipline, and community.

03

NA Programme Integration

Rebuild Movement complements the mental and spiritual work of the NA programme — adding the physical dimension recovery needs to be complete.

04

Build Real Connections

Train alongside people who truly understand what you're walking through. Community keeps people accountable when nothing else can.

05

Celebrate Every Win

Regular check-ins track your progress and celebrate the courage it takes to show up every day. Every step forward matters.

Our Vision

Africa's First Recovery
Fitness Network

"This isn't just a program. It's a movement."

South Africa has a massive gap in recovery aftercare. There is no scalable, community-driven fitness model. That's exactly what we're building — city by city, life by life.

We're raising funding to launch our first community in Johannesburg — to run sessions, build partnerships, and grow our base into something that can reach every city on the continent and beyond.

Johannesburg Launch

Our first community — establishing the model, building the base, proving the impact.

South Africa Expansion

Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria. Rebuild Centres in major cities across South Africa.

Corporate Wellness Partnerships

Partnering with companies to fund recovery fitness for their employees and communities.

Youth Prevention Programs

Taking fitness-based resilience into schools and youth communities before addiction takes hold.

Global Chapters

Africa's recovery fitness movement — taken to the world. One brick at a time.
